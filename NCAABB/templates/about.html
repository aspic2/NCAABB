About BracketBuilder

Why did you make this?

Hi, I'm Michael. I created BracketBuilder (BB) as a way to connect more with my sports-loving coworkers. While I do not care for sports, I do enjoy statistics and probability. A March Madness algorithm provides the perfect intersection of my interests and my coworkers.

I'm also looking for a job as a software developer, so it's nice to add some projects to my portfolio.

How do the picks work?

It's a lot simpler than you probably think. No machine learning (yet). No complicated statistics (yet). BB essentially picks teams the way that I would manually, but without the emotion and with much better precision.

BB uses a database of the season's games and scores and calculates a score for each team using four weighted parameters:

  • Number of games against Top 25 teams
  • Number of wins against Top 25 teams
  • Wins over the last 12 games
  • Overall season winning percentage
One day I may make the weighted parameters editable, but my defaults worked fine last year, and I don't know what overfitting is...
